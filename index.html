Ruben Santos

 

 

He is a professor, researcher and designer who studies and develops work in the field of Information Design. His work has been presented/ exhibited at Multimodus (2022), Reboot (2021, 2019), Ars Electronica (2020), NeurIPS (2020), ARTeFACTo (2020), Criatech (2020), xCoAx (2020, 2018), Reframed (2019) and ARTECH (2019). He holds a PhD with a research on Information Design for Explainable Artificial Intelligence, carried out at the UT Austin Portugal Program and at the NOVA LINCS research lab in Lisbon, where he continues to research. He also has a master's degree, in which he researched and developed data art artifacts, driven by raster image transcodings, and a bachelor's degree in Graphic and Multimedia Design. Teaches at the School of Arts and Design of Caldas da Rainha (ESADcr). Has also taught at IADE - Faculty of Design, Technology and Communication in Lisbon.
